Meet the advisory board

Our Skincare Advisory Board is comprised of renowned experts in chemistry and dermatology. Informed by the latest research, they guide our product innovation from initial formula to final glaze.

DR. RAMONA ROBINSON

cosmetic chemist

An innovator in the field, Dr. Robinson is an award-winning veteran cosmetic chemist with over 20 years experience in creating innovative, big-selling beauty products for leading beauty brands (Revlon, Avon, L’Oreal, Estée Lauder, Clinique) and developing products for top beauty brands. She founded the influencer agency, BeautyStack, where he has helped both start-up and large brands grow brand awareness, engagement, and product sales. Her clients include Shiseido, COTY, P&G Beauty, AHAVA, and Stila Cosmetics. Ramona recently started her own beauty brand, BeautyStack Cosmetics. She is also a resident beauty expert for (Allure magazine and Refinery29).

DR. DHAVAL AMIN

dermatologist

Dr. Dhaval Amin is a leading Dermatologist and digital health entrepreneur based in Manhattan. After finishing medical school, Dr. Amin completed his Dermatology residency at Mount Sinai hospital in New York City. He's been featured on ABC, NBC, CBS, New York Times, Elite Daily, Buzzfeed, Yahoo, Askmen, and countless other media outlets including People Magazine. His award winning research has been presented around the world and he currently serves as a medical expert for many companies in the field. He specializes in medical and cosmetic dermatology and looks to bridge the gap between cutting edge technologies and established paradigms.

DR. SHANE DARDEN

dermatologist

Considered the secret behind the most flawless faces, expert esthetician Shane Darden sets himself apart with his results-oriented approach to skin care. As a dermatologist-trained esthetician, Shane has learned hands-on how to deal with every kind of skin condition in an environment where the beauty standards are incredibly high. For over 20 years, Shane has been translating his knowledge and trusted expertise into developing efficacious formulas to under-complicate while delivering visible results. His award winning line, Shane Darden Skin Care is now his way to share his honest approach to skin with as many people as possible.
